# Case Studies

## Case Study 1: Turning Rookies into Daily Players (Fantasy Sports App)

### Problem
The fantasy sports market faced a significant retention cliff, with 95% of new players quitting before Day 30. Core user anxieties were identified as the root cause:
- **Choice Overload**: Too many options paralyzed new users
- **Fear of Losing Money**: Risk aversion prevented participation
- **Lack of Knowledge**: Users felt they didn't know enough to compete

### Approach
Shifted focus from competing on large prizes to building trust and reducing friction. Developed a user-centric solution addressing core anxieties:
- **Smart-Start**: A risk-free onboarding experience to eliminate choice overload
- **Built-in AI Coach (AI Quick-Scout)**: AI-powered feature providing data-driven suggestions to increase user confidence
- **Progression Engine**: Daily missions and streaks to foster long-term habit formation

### Key Metrics

| Metric | Industry Benchmark | Our Target |
|---|---|---|
| Day-1 Active Users | 32% | 45% |
| Day-30 Active Users | 7% | 12% |

### Impact
By addressing user anxiety, built a loyal user base. This approach led to significant improvement in retention metrics, making the app a compelling alternative in a crowded market.

## Case Study 2: Personalized Training for Every Athlete (Strava Training Plan)

### Problem
Users training for endurance events (marathons, triathlons) or specific fitness milestones lacked tailored, data-driven training plans that account for multiple sports and cross-training activities.

### Approach
Developed an AI-powered personalized training plan feature for Strava. This feature leverages user activity data across various sports (running, cycling, strength training) to create adaptive programs for different fitness goals.

### Key Feature Highlights
- **Adaptive Plans**: Tailored plans for goals like first 10K, half marathon, or full marathon
- **Cross-Training Integration**: Integrates activities like yoga and strength training for a holistic regimen
- **AI-Powered Insights**: Future scope included injury prediction and virtual coaching based on historical data

### Success Metrics
Measured through user adoption rates, training program completion rates, improvement in performance metrics (e.g., pace), and overall user retention.

### Impact
Differentiated Strava by offering a holistic, adaptive, and personalized experience. Helped users achieve their fitness goals more efficiently, driving higher completion rates and overall engagement with the platform and community. Abhishek's personal passion for running inspired this project.
