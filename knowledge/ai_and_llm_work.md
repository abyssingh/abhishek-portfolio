# AI and LLM Work

## Experience with AI, LLMs, and conversational products
I have worked on an AI smart assistant focused on shopping and support use cases in the Jio ecosystem. The product is designed to help users discover products and resolve issues through natural language interactions.

## Product thinking for AI
My approach emphasizes practical, high‑value use cases that reduce user effort and build trust. I focus on clearly defining what the assistant should and should not answer, and on prioritizing journeys where conversational guidance is most helpful.

## Evaluations, quality, trust, and UX
I align on response quality standards and UX expectations for AI outputs, including transparency when the assistant cannot answer a question. I also use product metrics and user feedback to guide iteration.

## Collaboration with engineering and ML teams
I work closely with engineering and ML partners to translate user needs into system requirements, coordinate delivery, and refine the experience based on performance signals.
