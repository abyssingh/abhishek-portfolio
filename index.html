Turning Rookies into Daily Players

Problem: The fantasy sports market faced a significant retention cliff, with 95% of new players quitting before Day 30. We identified core user anxieties as the root cause: Choice Overload, Fear of Losing Money, and Lack of Knowledge.

Approach: We shifted focus from competing on large prizes to building trust and reducing friction. We developed a user-centric solution to solve core anxieties.

  • Smart-Start: A risk-free onboarding experience to eliminate choice overload.
  • Built-in Coach: An AI-powered feature to provide data-driven suggestions and increase user confidence.
  • Progression Engine: Daily missions and streaks to foster long-term habit formation.

Impact: By addressing user anxiety, we built a loyal user base. This approach led to a significant improvement in retention metrics, making the app a compelling alternative in a crowded market.

Key Metrics Improvement:

Metric Benchmark Our Target
Day-1 Active Users 32% 45%
Day-30 Active Users 7% 12%

Personalized Training for Every Athlete

Problem: Users training for endurance events (like a marathon, which I know you love!) or specific milestones often lack tailored, data-driven training plans that account for multiple sports.

Approach: We developed an AI-powered personalized training plan feature for Strava. This feature leverages user activity data across various sports (running, cycling, strength training) to create adaptive programs for different fitness goals.

Key Feature Highlights:

  • Adaptive Plans: Tailored plans for goals like a first 10K, half marathon, or full marathon.
  • Cross-Training: Integrates activities like yoga and strength training for a holistic regimen.
  • AI-Powered Insights: Future scope included injury prediction and virtual coaching based on historical data.

Impact: This feature differentiated Strava by offering a holistic, adaptive, and personalized experience. It helped users achieve their fitness goals more efficiently, driving higher completion rates and overall engagement with the platform and community.

Success Metrics: We measured success through user adoption rates, training program completion rates, improvement in performance metrics (e.g., pace), and overall user retention.
